Pimp my Electric Kettle

Preparing yerba mate should be an enjoyable experience, from heating the water to taking that first sip... so I thought, why settle with my boring Walmart kettle when I have a microcontroller and LEDs lying around? So I fired up my IDE, did some coding, and soldered some components together, and now my temperature-controlled LEDs tell me when the water reaches perfect temperature for yerba mate

Meet B.O.M.SHELL

(Book Of Mormon Shell)

Request a demo!





BOMSHELL is a python-based scripture reference program that runs in your very own shell! It searches references from the Book of Mormon, Old Testament, and the New Testament at lightning speed, and can be run 100% local. While its functions are limited, good luck finding another scripture reference this light.
